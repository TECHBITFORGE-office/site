Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, absolutely. There are no lock-in contracts. You can cancel your subscription from your settings page at any time, and you will retain access until the end of your billing cycle.
Do plans include all AI features?
The Free plan includes basic AI generation models. To access advanced features like the API Builder, Website from Prompt, and Deep Debugging tools, you will need the Pro or Enterprise plan.
Is there a usage limit on the Pro plan?
The Pro plan offers unlimited AI code generation for personal use. We have a fair usage policy to prevent abuse (e.g., bot automation), but for normal development workflows, it is effectively unlimited.
